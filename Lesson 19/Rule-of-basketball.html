Important Rules of Basketball

  • Rule.1-The Player must bounce,or dribble,the ball with one hand while moving both feet.
  • Rule.2-The players hand must be on top of the ball while dribbling.
  • Rule.3-A team will consist of 12 players. Each team may have 5 players on the court at a time. If a team breaks this rule, they lose possession of the ball.
  • Rule.4-Score within the shot clock. ...
  • Rule.5-Ball and ballhandler must remain inbounds.
  • Rule.6-The offense has five seconds to inbound the ball. ...
